# RESPONSIBLE_DESIGN.md - Ethical AI, Privacy & Human-Review Safeguards

This document outlines the core privacy, ethical, and operational safeguards integrated into the FlowSense Liquidity & Risk Intelligence Platform. It details our human-in-the-loop review framework and false-positive handling, ensuring compliance with both technical standards and local regulatory guidelines in Bangladesh.

---

## 1. Schema-Level Privacy & Data Minimization

To uphold fair-market competition and prevent antitrust violations under the **Bangladesh Competition Commission** guidelines, the system operates with strict data minimization and isolation policies:
1.  **Strict Boundary Separation**: Competing providers (bKash, Nagad, and Rocket) maintain entirely independent data files (`bkash.csv`, `nagad.csv`, `rocket.csv`). No cross-provider database queries or relational tables are ever created.
2.  **No Cross-Linking of Identities**: Account numbers are tokenized into synthetic scoped IDs (e.g., `ACC-00042` under bKash). It is mathematically impossible for bKash Ops to link this token to Nagad's customer records, preventing customer multi-wallet surveillance.
3.  **Cryptographically Auditable Logins**: Every access to the aggregate crossover point is logged in `/db_files/audit_log.csv` and signed with a unique HMAC-SHA256 signature to prevent tampering.

---

## 2. Human-in-the-Loop Safeguards (No Automated Freezing)

An anomaly flag represents **statistical irregularity, never proof of fraud**. Systemic errors, hardware delays, and intense peak retail traffic often mimic velocity attacks. Therefore, the platform enforces **Strict Human-in-the-Loop Review**:

*   **Decision-Support Only**: The streaming anomaly engine is strictly an advisor. It generates alert cases but is **forbidden from executing automated financial actions** (such as freezing merchant balance accounts or locking agent registers).
*   **Manual Out-Of-Band Investigation**: Before any high-impact block or compliance freeze is requested from a provider, a human analyst must coordinate with the shop operator (Agent) and conduct an out-of-band verification (e.g., verifying physical paper registers or requesting confirmation via phone register).
*   **Auditable Case Notes**: No case can be closed, escalated, or resolved without recording explicit, human-input operational notes. This ensures accountability for every compliance decision.

## 3. False-Positive Mitigation during Peaks (Eid & Salary Cycles)

MFS usage in Bangladesh is highly seasonal, characterized by massive transaction spikes during:
1.  **Eid-ul-Fitr / Eid-ul-Adha Peaks**: Massive retail buying shifts cash flows dramatically over a 3-5 day window.
2.  **Monthly Salary Disbursements**: Heavy Cash-Out velocity spikes on the 1st-5th of every month.

### Seasonal Demand Adjustment (Dynamic Z-Scores)
A standard anomaly detector utilizing flat-average thresholds would flag thousands of benign transactions during these peaks, causing widespread false-alarm clutter and operational paralysis:
*   **Flat-Average Detector Peak False Alarms**: **1,453 instances** flagged during peak windows.
*   **Seasonally-Adjusted Online Detector False Alarms**: **969 instances** (549 during Eid and 420 during salary days).
*   **Mitigation Outcome**: Incorporating Phase 2's seasonal expected value baselines reduced false-alarm clutter by **33.3%** during high-traffic periods, preserving operational capacity and allowing compliance teams to focus on real structural threats.

## 4. Advisory Copy Guidelines (Non-Accusing Copy)

To protect customer dignity and avoid legal liabilities for wrongful accusations, all alert copy generated by the engine utilizes carefully crafted advisory language:
*   **Bengali/Banglish In-App Localized Copy**: Alerts are phrased to describe the physical phenomenon rather than accusing individuals of malice.
    *   *Incorrect (Accusatory)*: "Fraud transaction detected on bKash! Agent structure fraud."
    *   *Correct (Advisory / Phenomena)*: "Shorter interval transaction list checked on bKash. bKash cash-out balance drop continuous shift hobe. Please verify voice call record check table."
*   **Avoid Malicious Labels**: Labels like "fraudster", "money launderer", or "criminal" are strictly banned. Instead, the UI displays descriptive, measurable terms such as "repeated_amount (limits Structuring)", "sudden_burst (velocity spikes)", and "feed_conflict (ledger gap)".

These safeguards ensure that while super-agents leverage advanced statistical visibility, they do so with absolute respect for competition, privacy, and local operator trust.
